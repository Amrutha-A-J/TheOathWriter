About Me

My name is Adrian Abiel Martinez, I am an artist, writer and content creator. I use my time to create many things for entertaining and captivating others. I am from Miami Florida and have been a part of the growing art scene in Miami. Though I have created lots of art I take much more pleasure in constructing intricate worlds filled with lore and histories of its own.


I have created webcomics, maps, lore building journals, stories, and even created my own tabletop games. My most notable current work is “The Dark Journal” a horror series about a journal of monsters which can be found on the LINE WEBTOON website.


I am partially self-taught but also have been taught techniques from teachers and other students who are friends of mine. I have a goto attitude when it comes to making things for people to enjoy, I make sure it meets a certain standard even when I work with programs and techniques I am unfamiliar with. I do not limit myself to only what I know, I am always using new software and tools to help improve my works, and plan to learn how to use more. I use video editing software, photo editing software, animation software, and traditional techniques to tell my stories.
